				- **Progressive tax system**
					- Increasing tax rates with taxable base incomes \rarr rich pay higher taxes
				- **Average tax rate**
					- Total taxes divided by total income
				- **Marginal tax rate**
					- Rate paid on last dollar of income
					- Your tax bracket corresponds to your marginal tax rate
						- Higher than average tax rate because of the progressive tax system
						- ![image.png](../assets/image_1763173051196_0.png){:height 247, :width 436}
						- ![image.png](../assets/image_1763173149385_0.png){:height 218, :width 367}
						- ![Income percentage vs Share of federal taxes](../assets/image_1763173176949_0.png){:height 384, :width 498}
				- **Proportional tax system**
					- System in which everyone pays the exact same proportion of their income in taxes
					- Ex: Marginal and average tax are the same for everyone
				- **Regressive tax system**
					- System in which the lower the income the higher percentage of income paid in taxes
					- Ex: Marginal and average tax rates decrease as the income rises
					- ![image.png](../assets/image_1763173967490_0.png){:height 352, :width 523}
				- **Tax incidence**
					- Statutory (legal) incidence vs. economic (true) tax incidence
					- Who bears the burden of the application of the tax?
						- ![image.png](../assets/image_1763174215384_0.png){:height 170, :width 513}
						- Who actually pays the tax (economic incidence)?
							- Consumers due to high prices
							- Producers via lower prices
