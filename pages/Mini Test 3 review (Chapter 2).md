- # Recording Transactions
  id:: 691d0ac1-47e2-4526-b994-107597640e36
	- **Materials**
		- **Using materials in production**
			- **Debit:** Work in Process inventory (for Direct Materials) or Manufacturing Overhead (for Indirect Materials)
			- **Credit:** Raw Materials inventory
	- **Labor**
		- **Recording labor costs**
			- **Debit:** Work in Process inventory (for Direct Labor) or Manufacturing Overhead (for Indirect Labor)
			- **Credit:** Wages Payable (or Cash)
	- **Manufacturing Overhead**
		- **Recording actual overhead costs (e.g., Depreciation)**
			- **Debit:** Depreciation expense
			- **Credit:** Accumulated Depreciation
		- **Allocating overhead to jobs**
			- **Debit:** Work in Process inventory
			- **Credit:** Manufacturing Overhead
	- **Completion and Sale**
		- **Transferring completed jobs**
			- **Debit:** Finished Goods Inventory
			- **Credit:** Work in Process Inventory
		- **Selling the product (Cost flow)**
			- **Debit:** Cost of Goods Sold
			- **Credit:** Finished Goods Inventory
	- **Year-End Adjustments**
		- **Adjusting Under/Overallocated Overhead**
			- **Debit/Credit:** Manufacturing Overhead (to close the account balance to zero)
			- **Debit/Credit:** Cost of Goods Sold (amount needed to balance the entry)
